Launched a Full Finance Operation in 12 Months | Enabling Commercial Scale-Up

Tanzania | 2016–2017 | Philip Morris International (PMI)

Dropped into a true greenfield environment, tasked with building the finance backbone for a new market entry. I oversaw design, financial planning, and alignment with PMI global standards, plus ensuring the right support from the Cape Town hub and access to HQ expertise.

Within 12 months:

  • Core finance processes — R2R (Record to Report), P2P (Procure to Pay), O2C (Order to Cash), payroll and internal control were implemented.
  • Finance team of 6 FTE was trained, structured, and steadily progressing toward PMI global standards
  • SAP FI was implemented and stabilized
  • Launched product sales in Dar es Salaam (pop. 5.4M) via newly contracted national distributor.

→ The result: A complete, compliant, and scalable finance operation — built from scratch and enabling rapid commercial growth.

Built and Deployed Odoo ERP Across 17 Entities | Enabling Real-Time Finance and Operations

Switzerland | 2021–2022 | VESPER Group

Led the end-to-end design and implementation of Odoo ERP. I taught myself the platform, built the accounting framework, integrated banking systems, and partnered with the COO to deliver non-finance modules.

Within 6 months, Accounting and HR modules were configured and deployment-ready, while Sales, Purchasing, and Inventory were fully designed. During project close, I ensured full integration across all modules and supported the final go-live across 17 global entities.

→ The result: A unified, cloud-based ERP — enabling one system, process discipline, and scalable operations.

→ Key learning: Even in smaller organizations, change management must be embedded from the start to ensure lasting adoption.

Restructured the Business to Unlock Critical Liquidity

Switzerland | 2022–2023 | ELDICO Scientific

Faced with rising financial pressure, I restructured the company into two legal entities — a service arm and an R&D unit — strategically reframing the financial narrative to highlight the strength of the services business. This enabled me to secure a bank loan equivalent to 20% of the company's equity, completed within just four weeks of negotiation.

The funding provided six months of runway, safeguarded payroll for a 10-person team, and allowed the company to pay critical operational invoices. The board welcomed the solution.

→ The result: A bold move, a clear message, and the liquidity needed to fuel what came next.
